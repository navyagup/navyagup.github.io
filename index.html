Navya Gupta

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Physics at the University of Maryland, College Park, advised by Prof. Zohreh Davoudi. My research lies at the intersection of quantum information, lattice gauge theory, and tensor‑network algorithms. I develop quantum‑computing protocols for quantum field theories and classical tensor‑network methods for strongly interacting many‑body systems.
